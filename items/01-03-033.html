Title:
Corner of First and Main Streetsin Moscow, Idaho
Sort Date:
1916
Date Created Start:
1916
Description:
This is an exterior view of a building which had been remodeled about 1916. The note on the back of this image reads "The front [of the building] has been changed into one room for displaying automobiles. The side wasll has been cut and a large door was put in. Sullivan and Reilley rent the entire building for Ford and Dodge car sales. The building rents for $100 per month."
